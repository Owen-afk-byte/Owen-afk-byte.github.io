About Me

Let’s see, who am I? I suppose, if I were being generous, I could classify myself with a lot of labels: student, programmer, tech-enthusiast, retail worker, artist, designer, builder, crafter, minecrafter, jokester, burnt-out, disaster, motivated, lazy, moody, enthusiastic, tired, friendly, helpful, anxious, selfish, selfless, intelligent, stupid, late, early, punctual, incredible, boring, aimless, and determined. And that’s just on Monday's.

Perhaps, this would be better told as an amalgamation of what I do right now, as opposed to who I might possibly think I am, from the perspective of a consciousness that has the luxury of perceiving every stray thought that may puncture my waking hours, as I stumble through the world making numerous mistakes, hopefully falling upward into some life of self imposed meaning. *gasp* This is harder than I thought.

Right now as I sit here, on Wednesday the 9th of November 2022 at 12:12, I have one main occupation that takes up the majority of my time: student life. Currently, I study computer science at The University of Edinburgh. However, I won’t be here much longer, this is my last year of four. Scary thought I know. In these (almost) three and a half years here, I have questioned what I wish to do with my future and if CS is the path for me. You see, the design of such courses at a university with such prestige leaves a lot to be desired. The main thing being FUN.

So if I am to truthfully answer what it is that I have learned in my time here, it wouldn’t be anything as straightforward as object-oriented programming or the foundations of data science. I decided that if I am going to continue intentional learning for the rest of my life, as I intend to do, then I should learn and have fun while doing it. I should LAHF. Because what is the point in life without a little laughter?

That’s what this is for - this is the fun bit. I am now seeking out to be as creative as I have always been, just with a CS twist. The expectation is that I will continue building this website, amongst other projects, some CS related and some not, in a venture to enjoy what I have learned previously and what I wish to learn going forward. And I want to LAHF while doing it.

Also, I work in a supermarket, I guess.
